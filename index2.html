Our Problem

This is the Problem that we have deided to tackle and found the perfect solution

In many communities around the world, access to clean and safe water is becoming a critical issue. Water scarcity is not only caused by environmental factors such as droughts but is also worsen by poor water management, inefficient consumption, and pollution. In some urban areas, water wastage is also a significant problem, with people unaware of how much water they are consuming daily.

More About The Problem

Water scarcity is a critical global issue, affecting billions and posing significant challenges to health, agriculture, and economic development.

Current Global Situation

  • Population Impact: Approximately 4 billion people experience severe water scarcity for at least one month each year.

  • Economic Implications: By 2025, 1.8 billion people are projected to live in countries with absolute water scarcity, potentially leading to significant economic and social challenges.

    • Contributing Factors

  • Climate Change: Rising global temperatures have led to increased evaporation rates and altered precipitation patterns, intensifying droughts and reducing freshwater availability.

  • Population Growth: The global population has increased significantly, leading to higher water demand for domestic, agricultural, and industrial uses.

  • Urbanization: Rapid urban expansion often outpaces the development of water infrastructure, leading to inefficient water use and increased pollution.

    Our Solution

    AquaSmart is a mobile app that helps individuals, families, and communities monitor and reduce water consumption through smart tracking, data analytics, and educational resources, promoting informed decisions and collective action for water conservation.
